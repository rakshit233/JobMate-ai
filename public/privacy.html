Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how JobMate AI ("JobMate", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our web application at app.jobmate.tech (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data controller

Rakshit Tiwari · JobMate AI
Contact: hello@jobmate.tech

1. What data we collect

We collect only what is needed to provide the Service:

  • Account data — your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Google or via an email magic link.
  • Profile & application content — information you choose to enter, such as your CV/resume details, work experience, education, skills, and job descriptions you paste in for tailoring.
  • Usage data — anonymous, aggregated analytics about how the Service is used (for example, page views and feature clicks). This does not identify you personally and does not use tracking cookies.

2. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account and let you sign in.
  • To generate tailored CVs, cover letters, interview preparation, salary guidance, and job-match scores based on the information you provide.
  • To save your profiles, resume versions, and job-tracker entries so they are available when you return.
  • To understand, in aggregate, how the Service is used so we can improve it.

The legal basis for this processing is the performance of our service to you (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) and, for analytics, our legitimate interest in improving the Service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4. AI processing

When you use AI features, the text you submit (such as your profile details and a job description) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This is used only to produce your result and is not used by us to train AI models.

5. Data retention

We keep your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual profiles, resume versions, and tracker entries at any time within the app. If you would like your entire account and associated data deleted, email us at hello@jobmate.tech and we will remove it.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, to restrict or object to its processing, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@jobmate.tech. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

7. Data security

Your data is stored with row-level security so that each user can only access their own records. We use reputable infrastructure providers and industry-standard measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

8. Cookies

We use only the essential cookies required to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. Our analytics are cookieless and anonymous.

9. Children

The Service is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

For any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at hello@jobmate.tech.
